# Online-Store-Data-Analysis(Intractive dashboard creation using MS excel)
## project objectives
 the store wants to create an anual sales report for the analyzation of the performance of sales and all other components.
 the owner of the store can understand their customers and grow more sales in next year.

 ## Dataset used 
 -<a href="https://github.com/Vineettyagi1/DATA-ANALYSIS-DASHBOARD/blob/main/dashboard%20project.xlsx">dataset</a>

 ## QUESTIONS (KPIs)
   The following metrics and insights are presented as key performance indicators "KPIs" that a business would want to track:
 - compare the sales and order using single chart.
 - which year got the highest sales and orders?
 - total sales.
 - Sales by Category and Sales by Sub-Category
 - Profit Gain Over Time
 - Monthly Sales Performance
 - Top 5 Customers by Profit
 - Sales by State/Location
 - Customer Count by Year

 - Dashboard intrection <a href="https://github.com/Vineettyagi1/DATA-ANALYSIS-DASHBOARD/blob/main/Screenshot%202026-01-30%20092720.png">Dashboard</a>

## Project insight
- The dashboard helps track how total sales and profit have changed over specific periods, such as from 2014 to 2017.
- It allows for analysis of sales and profit distribution across different product categories like Furniture, Office Supplies, and Technology.
- Users can observe how sales fluctuate month-to-month within different years, identifying periods of increase or decrease.
- The dashboard highlights the top customers who contribute most to the profit, enabling businesses to focus on valuable client relationships.
- It provides a detailed view of which sub-categories within broader categories generate the most sales.
- It provides a detailed view of which sub-categories within broader categories generate the most sales.
- The dashboard can show the count of unique customers over different years, indicating growth or decline in the customer base.
- By making the dashboard interactive with slicers, users can filter data by year or category to gain specific insights quickly and efficiently.

## Final Conclusion:
The project concludes by demonstrating how to transform a raw, unorganized sales dataset into a fully interactive and visually appealing Excel dashboard. this project teaches:
Build a comprehensive sales dashboard .
Utilize various Excel functionalities, including pivot tables, charts, and slicers, to create an interactive tool for data analysis.
Gain valuable business insights from data that would otherwise be "boring" and difficult to interpret.
The final dashboard allows for dynamic filtering by year and category, providing immediate insights into sales trends, profit gains, customer performance, and geographical distribution. This end-to-end project serves as a practical example of how data can be prepared, analyzed, and presented effectively for decision-making.
